Social Justice Inequality

Social Implications

The event that occured in the summer of 2020, beginning with the murder of civilian George Floyd, reminded America of the need for social justice equality in our country. Unfortunately, everyone does not share the same views on this topic which caused a major divide in our country that is still affecting people now.

Economic Implications

One huge economical change surrounding this topic includes the increased support of black owned business. One way people showed their support for black people and their communities was by shiftinf theri focus to black owned businesses and choosing to spend their money there instead of the alternative options. Because of this, black owned businesses are booming more now than ever before.

Political Implications

With the presidential election, coming up there has been a lot of tension surrounding voting and which candidate gets elected. There has also been an incredible increase in the support of voting. The events surrounding social justice inequality has encourage many people to register and vote in the upcoming election. Everyone make sure to cast your vote this year so we can make steps towards social justice equality!
