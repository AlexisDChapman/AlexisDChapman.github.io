Our Mission

The Greeley Dream Team is committed to helping students succeed in education and in life.

Vision

Through multiple resources, we reach as many students in the Greeley community as possible. We make it our mission to help all students, including students from low income families, find a way to graduate high school and attend college. Through programs, partnerships, and grants we hope to inspire kids and help them achieve the best education possible.

7. Continued Educational Support, Following High School Graduation
